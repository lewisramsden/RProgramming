Week2 Helpful Tools

Of course, these are the vital foundations to any analysis conducted in R and are the most important to learn and remember. That being said, there are three additional tools that you should be made aware of and, in fact, from this point on should implement at all times when using R.

We believe that the content discussed above is a good introduction to R and its many features and we hope you have found it useful. However, in order to really make the most of R and for your to conduct a meaningful analysis, you will have to understand each of the above components (and many more) in much greater detail. In the following chapters, we delve into each topic to learn more about them and provide a number of different exercises for you to try yourselves. After all, the best way to improve you programming skills is for you to do it on your own.
